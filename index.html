About G K KOTECHA & ASSOCIATES

Who We Are

Established in 2017, G K Kotecha & Associates is a firm of Company Secretaries in Practice providing comprehensive legal, secretarial, and governance services. We assist companies, LLPs, start-ups, and NGOs in meeting their statutory and regulatory requirements effectively.

Our approach blends professional expertise, integrity, and technology to deliver timely and reliable solutions tailored to each client’s needs.

Our Objective

To Simplify governance and compliance for every business while fostering a culture of accountability and transparency.

Vision

To simplify the governance of every business through the aid of technology globally.

Mission

To build a platform that delivers professional legal and compliance services by expert practitioners, enabling businesses to focus on growth while we handle governance.
